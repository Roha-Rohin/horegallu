In the moments of our life,
we may happen to see some eyes
searching for something. And
Yesterday I happen to see a child with his father. They
were looking ar the same thing
hands crossed due to respect
As the child hold his father's
hand he was asking something to
his dather. I can see the
father nodding his head for all the questions.
And I saw, They were looking at
a red Royal Enfeild bike. The
rider wearing a level 1 vest.
an LS2 Sparky helmet.

The dream of a Child.
The dream for a father. I wondered
what did they have thought at that moment
Did the child asked his father
to buy one?
Did the father nodded by
knowing that as an unreachable dream?

And at that moment I realised
by starting my father's bike
when the signal trned green.
May be I was once that child
and my father would have nodded
The Father who fulfilled his child's dream. And some day that father will do the same.
