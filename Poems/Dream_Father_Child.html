Dream
A Father & A Child

Inspiration

In the moments of our life,
We may happen to see some eyes
Searching for something.
Yesterday I happen to see a child with his father.
They were looking ar the same thing
Hands crossed in respect
As the child hold his father's hand
He was asking something to his father.
I can see the father nodding his head for all the questions.
And I saw, They were looking at a red Royal Enfeild bike.
The rider wearing a level 1 vest, a LS2 Sparky helmet.

The dream of a Child.
The dream for a father. I wondered
what did they have thought at that moment
Did the child asked his father
to buy one?
Did the father nodded by
knowing that as an unreachable dream?

And at that moment I realised
by starting my father's bike
when the signal trned green.
May be I was once that child
and my father would have nodded
The Father who fulfilled his child's dream.
And some day that father will do the same.
