Who is
Horegallu

"Hearing is listening to what is said
Listening is hearing what isn't said."

When searching for the meaning of Horegallu, found that “A stone that can bear weight”. The traveller who steps kilometres and miles into the untold mysteries of life needs such a stone. A stone who can hold their burden for a small time. A stone to which they can smile and cry at the same time. And where the strings of memories are controlled by time, life is meaningless without Horegallus.

This is the Diary written by one of those Horegallus.
